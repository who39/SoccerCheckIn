# Soccer Check-in — Help

Welcome! This guide walks you through everything you can do in the Soccer Check-in app. No tech background needed — just follow the steps.

---

## Sign in

1. Go to the site URL your coach or admin shared with you.
2. Click **Login** (top-right).
3. Pick the Microsoft account you want to use. Use the same one every time so the app recognises you.

> Tip: Whoever signs you up to a Program will use this email, so let your coach know which one you'll log in with.

---

## Programs

A **Program** is your team or season (for example, "Spring U10 Lions").

- Click **Programs** in the menu to see every program you belong to.
- Click a program name to see its **events** (practices, games), the **roster of players**, and any **announcements**.

If you don't see your program listed, ask your coach for an invite link or to add you by email.

---

## Players (your kids)

- From a program page, click **Players** to see the kids you can check in for.
- Click **+ Add Player** to register your child. Enter their name and any notes (e.g. "shin-guards in red bag").
- You can **Edit** or **Delete** a player at any time.

> A player is registered to one program at a time. If your child is in two programs, add them once in each.

---

## Family — share check-in with another parent

This is what to use when **both parents** (or a parent and a grandparent, or a babysitter) need to drop the kid off and check them in.

### What is a family?

A **family** is a small group of users who share the same kids. Anyone in the family can check those kids in or out for any event. One person — the **family head** — manages who's in the family.

### Create your family (one-time)

1. Click **Family** in the menu.
2. Type a name (usually your last name, e.g. "Smith") and click **Create family**.

That's it — you're now the family head, and any players you've already added are automatically attached to the family.

### Add another parent (or guardian)

You have two options on the **Family** page:

**Option A — by email (simplest):**
- Type the other person's email and click **Add**.
- If they've already signed in to the app before, they're added immediately.
- If they haven't, the app will give you an **invite link** to share — text or email it to them.

**Option B — invite link:**
- Click **+ New invite** to generate a shareable link.
- Send it to anyone you want to add. The first person who signs in and opens the link joins your family.

> Invites expire after **14 days**. You can revoke any pending invite at any time.

### Joining a family someone sent you

1. Click the invite link they shared.
2. Sign in with your Microsoft account if asked.
3. You're in! You'll now see all of that family's players when you go to **Players** or check in at an event.

### Removing a member

The family head can remove anyone (except themselves) from the **Family** page using the **Remove** button.

### Deleting the family

Only the head can delete the family. Players stay in the system but are no longer shared — the original creator can re-attach them by creating a new family.

> **Note:** You can only be in **one family at a time**. If you need to switch families, leave or delete the current one first.

---

## Check in for an event

1. Click **Programs** → pick your program → click an event.
2. For each player, click **Going** or **Not going**.
3. To change your mind, click again or use **Reset**.

Anyone in the family — head or member — can check in any of the family's players. Your selection is saved instantly; no "Submit" button needed.

## Common questions

**Q: I added my partner by email but it says "no account found."**
The app needs them to sign in once first. Either ask them to sign in, then try again, or send them the invite link the app generated for you — that link will work even before they have an account.

**Q: My kid plays in two programs. Do I need two families?**
No. Family is **global** — one family covers all programs. As long as the player is registered in each program, anyone in the family can check them in for either program's events.

**Q: I checked the wrong button.**
Just click the right one, or click **Reset** to clear your selection. The latest click wins.

**Q: I want someone to help check kids in but not manage the roster.**
Add them as a family member. Members can check in but can't add or remove other members — only the head can.

**Q: I'm not seeing the Family menu.**
You need to be **signed in**. If you are signed in and still don't see it, refresh the page.

**Q: I lost the invite link I created.**
Open the **Family** page — your active invites are listed there. You can copy the link or revoke and create a new one.

## Need help?

If something isn't working, contact your program coach or admin. They can check whether your account is set up correctly and re-send invites.
